Restricted Items

The following cannot be transported:

  • Hazardous materials — flammable liquids, explosives, radioactive substances
  • Illegal substances or stolen goods
  • Live animals or pets
  • Firearms, ammunition, or weapons of any kind
  • Human remains
  • Perishable food requiring temperature control
  • High-value items over £2,000 without prior written agreement
  • Cash, bank cards, or negotiable instruments
  • Asbestos or toxic materials
  • Items exceeding the van's stated dimension or weight limits
⚠️ Knowingly misdeclaring prohibited items may result in cancellation without refund and referral to the relevant authorities.
